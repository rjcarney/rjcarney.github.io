Transform Any Space Into An Oasis.

Based in Tuscon, Arizona, Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm that specializes in garden design and creation, maintenance and landscaping. The company also runs a small plant nursery that sells indoor and outdoor plants, making them a one-stop shop for clients.

Inside and Out

Our team of landscapers can turn any space into an Oasis that you can be proud of. Custom designs to meet the aesthetic of your life. Whether bringing hints of nature inside or transforming the landscape into your own Oasis.

Stop and Smell the Roses

Or any of our other wide variety of lush folage that is growing in our nursery. Bouqets, baskets, potted plants, shrubs, and trees. We have all the plants to meet your needs. We can even help you accomplish your landscaping vision.

Thank Our Lucky Shrubs

Lucky Shrub was started by a husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants. Jason, garden architect, creates and oversees all the landscaping designs. Maria manages all marketing for
the company and runs the nursery.
